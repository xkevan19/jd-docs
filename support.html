Common Issues & Solutions

Browse through our frequently reported issues and their solutions

Account Login Problems

Unable to access your account? Try these solutions:

  • Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser
  • Ensure your account hasn't been suspended

Payment Processing Issues

If you're experiencing problems with payments:

  • Verify your payment method is up to date
  • Check if your card hasn't expired
  • Ensure your billing address matches your card details
  • Contact your bank to see if transactions are being blocked

Application Performance

If the application is running slowly:

  • Update to the latest version of the software
  • Restart the application and your device
  • Check your internet connection speed
  • Close other resource-intensive applications

Data Sync Problems

If your data isn't syncing properly:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify sync is enabled in settings
  • Force a manual sync through the application
  • Ensure you haven't exceeded storage limits
